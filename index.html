About

Ms. Sorsia Muttarak, M.Sc. is a Quality Assurance Nurse and Risk Management Nurse at Bangkok Hospital Phuket. Her professional interests include nutrition, healthcare risk management, and hospital quality standards (JCI and HA). She specializes in data analysis, risk management, document control, and applied nutritional science.
